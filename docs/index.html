Privacy Policy

Miwok App  |  Last updated: April 15, 2026

Introduction

Thank you for using the Miwok App. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use it. The Miwok App is a language-learning application that helps users learn vocabulary words in the Miwok language through numbers, colors, family members, and common phrases — presented with images and audio.

Information We Collect

The Miwok App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account registration or login is required.
  • No personal data (name, email, location, etc.) is collected.
  • No usage analytics or tracking is performed.
  • No data is sent to any server or third party.

Permissions

The app requests only the permissions necessary to play audio pronunciations of Miwok words. No sensitive device permissions (camera, microphone, contacts, location, etc.) are used.

Third-Party Services

The Miwok App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics services. No data is shared with any third party.

Children's Privacy

The Miwok App is suitable for all ages, including children. Because no personal information is collected, the app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the new version will be published at this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository .
